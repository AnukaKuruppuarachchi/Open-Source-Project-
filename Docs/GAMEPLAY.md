# Gameplay Guide

## 1. Overview
- Capture The Flag is one of the arena modes in Hypersomnia.
- Two teams fight to steal the enemy flag and bring it back to their base.
- First team to reach 3 wins (adjustable in the map editor).

## 2. Your Base and Your Flag
Each team has:
- A base marked on the map (A = Resistance, B = Metropolis)
- A flag that spawns at the base at the start of each round

Flag can be:
- At base
- Carried
- Dropped (instantly returns)

## 3. How to Capture
To score a capture:
1. Steal the enemy flag
2. Bring it back to your base
3. Your flag must be at your base
4. Step inside your base zone


If all conditions are met, your team scores 1 capture.

## 4. Picking Up and Dropping Flags
- Picking up your flag returns it instantly
- Picking up the enemy flag lets you carry it
- If you die while carrying a flag, it resets

## 5. Scoring Points

These points are just for your individual stats and scoreboard. 

They do not decide who wins, the match is won by the first team to reach 3 captures.

Players earn individual points by:

- Capture: 5 points
- Knockout: 2 points
- Assist: 1 point

## 6. How a Match Ends
A match ends when:
- A team reaches 3 captures (defualt captures) or whichever amount was set
- Half time is reached
- Final round ends

## 7. Movement Controls
- W: Move around
- A: Move left
- D: Move right
- S: Move backwards
- Mouse: Look around
- Space: Jump

## 8. How to Pick Up a Flag
- Enemy Flag: Press E while standing on it
- Your Flag: Press E to return it to your base
